Cauliflower Scampi
.

Share :

Cook Time: 1 Hour
Servings: 4
Ingredients: 8

Health Benefits
  • Ashwagandha improves mood and stress
  • Prebiotics support a healthy gut and immune system
  • Omega 3’s promote a healthy brain and cardiovascular system
Ingredients
  • 1 box Banza Cascatelli
  • 1½ cups tightly packed basil leaves, plus additional for garnish
  • ½ cup tightly packed kale leaves, stems removed
  • ¼ cup walnuts, plus extra for garnish
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan, plus extra for garnis
  • ¼ cup whole milk ricotta or mascarpone
  • Juice of 1 lemon
Directions
  • 1Blanch Greens: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Season generously with salt. Add basil and kale leaves, and cook for 10 seconds, or until bright green in color. Remove with tongs or a strainer, reserving water for next step. Rinse under cold water until cool, then squeeze out excess water.
  • 2Cook Banza: Add Banza Cascatelli to boiling water and cook according to package directions. Strain pasta with cold water and set aside.
Tips
  • Note: this is meant to be served cold.
  • Not into walnuts? Sub almonds, hazelnuts, peanuts, pepitas, or sunflower seeds—or a mixture of several varieties.
