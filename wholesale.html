Who We Are

We create healthy, happy plant based products for those who want to live well, be well, and eat well. Our founder and Chief Visionary Officer is an Army Veteran thriving with PTSD who discovered the benefits of holistic healthy eating for wellness and wellbeing. Our story is crafted around strength from adversity and using holistic alternatives to promote good health and wellbeing. We are on a mission to advocate for mental health and build healthy, happy communities through our Greenios Community Garden Initiative. We take pride in our products and love what we do. We believe it is our duty to make the world a more happier place through our healthy, happy foods!
